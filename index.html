Who is Mr. Hoffman

This is my 20th year teaching at Hilbert High School. I teach our upper level language arts courses and our computer science courses.

In addition to English 11, I teach several senior-level dual-credit classes. Two are offered through St. Norbert College and two through Fox Valley Technical College. Both options allow seniors to earn college credits and while taking high school classes.

I’ve also recently begun teaching computer science classes in association with the TEALS program offered by Microsoft Philanthropies. TEALS connects schools with CS professionals who work as volunteer instructors, helping to teach computer science concepts to students. We offer an introductory computer science class as well as advanced computer science and web development.

I received my bachelor's degree in 2000 from the UW-Green Bay and my master's in English from UW-Oshkosh in 2007.
